Exploring the Estonian Footprint in Norway: A 2023 Overview

A Personal Journey to Uncover Our Roots in the North

As an Estonian who has called Norway home for nearly five years, my curiosity about the presence of my fellow countrymen in this beautiful land grew each day. Today, I'm excited to share with you a detailed visualization of the Estonian community spread across Norway.

Discovering the Numbers

My quest began at Statistisk sentralbyrå (Statistics Norway), where I unearthed intriguing data about our small but vibrant community. The journey continued with GeoNorge, providing vital map shape information of municipalities and counties, crucial for my analysis.

Reflections: More Than Just Numbers

These visualizations are not just about numbers; they represent our stories, struggles, and triumphs in a foreign land. From the bustling streets of Oslo to the serene landscapes of Nordland, each Estonian has a unique tale to tell.

The Story Behind the Data

What fascinated me was not only the distribution but also the stories these numbers might be silently narrating. How have my fellow Estonians adapted to life here? What challenges and opportunities did they encounter?
